The house

Ten minutes from the most authentic fishing village in the bay. Terrace facing Marina Riviera Nayarit, two bedrooms opening to the garden, kitchen equipped for a chef.

Canadian snowbirds come back year after year. It's the rare mix between real quiet and full services within reach — gym and three pools in the complex, the seafood market on Wednesdays eight minutes on foot, fiber WiFi that holds up to video calls with family in Toronto.

One honest tip: if you stay more than two weeks, we'll send you to the village laundry — does a better job than the building's.
